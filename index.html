About Me

EUR

Edgar Ulises Rojo Rodriguez

Mechatronics Engineer · M.Sc. & Ph.D. Candidate in Aeronautical Engineering

Mechatronics Engineer with a M.Sc. and Ph.D. candidacy in Aeronautical Engineering. Over 5 years of experience in control systems design, industrial metrology, advanced manufacturing, and applied research. I have led multidisciplinary projects for Tier-1 automotive suppliers (Magna Powertrain, FEMSA, Friedrich), directed internationally funded research (ONR Global), and completed a research fellowship at the University of Southampton, UK.

Currently an Associate Professor at FIME-UANL teaching Electrical Systems, Measurement Techniques, and Aerodynamics, with active involvement in the Metrology Lab, Advanced Manufacturing Lab, and Navigation research area. I mentor students through terminal projects and extracurricular engineering teams while providing direct technical services to industry.

My work bridges physical systems with intelligent automation — from control logic development and instrumentation to AI-driven workflows using Python, MATLAB, and modern web technologies.
