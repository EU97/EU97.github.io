SISELA-Init

Introducción a los Sistemas Eléctricos de Aeronaves — Introduction to Aircraft Electrical Systems

View on GitHub

Overview

SISELA-Init is an open educational resource covering the fundamentals of aircraft electrical systems. Developed for use in aeronautical engineering courses at FIME-UANL, the repository provides Python-based simulations, Jupyter Notebooks, and reference materials aligned with real aircraft electrical architectures.

The name stands for SIstemas Eléctricos de Aeronaves (Aircraft Electrical Systems), and the "Init" module covers introductory topics for undergraduate students.

Topics Covered

  • AC and DC aircraft power systems fundamentals
  • Electrical bus architecture and load distribution
  • Generator and battery system modeling
  • Electrical load analysis and circuit protection
  • ATA 24 compliance concepts for aircraft electrical systems
  • Python simulations for fault analysis and system behavior

Academic Context

Used as supplementary material in the Electrical Systems course taught by Prof. Edgar Ulises Rojo Rodriguez at FIME-UANL. The Python notebooks enable students to explore and simulate aircraft electrical behavior interactively, bridging theory and practice.
